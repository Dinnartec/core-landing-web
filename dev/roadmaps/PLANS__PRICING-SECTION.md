### 2. Optimization Sprint

**Purpose:** Execute concrete solutions in short, focused cycles.

| Tier | Price | Duration | Best for |
|------|-------|----------|----------|
| Sprint S | $1,500 USD | 2 weeks | Single automation or integration |
| Sprint M | $3,000 USD | 3-4 weeks | Multiple automations or small AI implementation |
| Sprint L | $5,000 USD | 4-6 weeks | Complex integrations or custom development |

**What's included:**
- Defined scope (agreed before start)
- Design + implementation
- Testing and deployment
- Documentation
- 2 weeks of bug fixes after delivery

**Scope examples:**

*Sprint S ($1,500):*
- One workflow automation (n8n, Make, Zapier)
- One API integration
- One chatbot or AI assistant (basic)
- One internal tool (simple)

*Sprint M ($3,000):*
- 2-3 connected automations
- AI implementation with custom logic
- Dashboard or reporting tool
- Multi-system integration

*Sprint L ($5,000):*
- End-to-end process automation
- Custom web application (MVP)
- Complex AI pipeline
- Full system integration (3+ tools)

## FAQ

**Q: What if the Diagnosis doesn't find opportunities?**
A: We refund 50% or apply full amount to a Sprint if you proceed.

**Q: Can we start without Diagnosis?**
A: Yes, if scope is already clear. Diagnosis is recommended but not mandatory.

**Q: What if a Sprint takes longer than expected?**
A: We commit to the scope, not the hours. We deliver what's agreed.

**Q: Do you work with early-stage startups?**
A: Yes. Sprint S and M are designed for lean budgets.

**Q: Can we pause a Retainer?**
A: Yes, with 15 days notice. No pause longer than 2 months.
